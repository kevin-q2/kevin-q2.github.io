About Me

I am a PhD student at Boston University where I am advised by Evimaria Terzi. I am currently researching new ways of using data to understand structure and dynamics in the spread of Covid-19. I am very interested by geometric or graphical structures which arise from certain natural/biological processes (such as diseases/epidemics), and what they can tell us about any underlying dynamical processes. More broadly I am very concerned by how data science and machine learning are affecting society, and I find it important to think about how they might be used responsibly. And even more broadly I am still just trying to figure out what I'm interested in!
